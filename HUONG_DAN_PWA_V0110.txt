DWG SKETCH PWA EDITOR V0.11.0
====================================

1. KHONG mo index.html bang file:// neu muon cai PWA/cap nhat.
2. Thu nghiem tren Windows:
   - Chay serve_local.cmd
   - Mo http://localhost:8080/index.html
   - Bam "Cai PWA" neu trinh duyet hien nut.
3. Android:
   - Dua toan bo thu muc PWA len may chu HTTPS noi bo/cong cong.
   - Mo URL bang Chrome/Edge va chon Cai ung dung/Them vao man hinh chinh.
4. Kiem tra cap nhat:
   - Nut "Kiem tra phien ban" doc version.json.
   - Khi version.json co version lon hon, nut "Cap nhat ngay" xuat hien.
   - Can thay dong bo index.html, sw.js, manifest va cac tep lien quan tren may chu.
   - Moi ban phat hanh phai doi APP_VERSION/CACHE_NAME trong sw.js va version trong version.json.
5. Dia chi cap nhat:
   - Mac dinh ./version.json cung may chu.
   - Co the nhap URL version.json khac trong khung "PWA va cap nhat".
   - Neu khac ten mien, may chu phai cho phep CORS.
6. Du an JSON van duoc chon bang nut Mo JSON va duoc xu ly noi bo tren thiet bi.
