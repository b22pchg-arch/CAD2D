DWG SKETCH PWA V0.13.0

1. CHON NHIEU THEO VUNG
- Chon cong cu Chon.
- Keo tu trai sang phai: Window, chi chon doi tuong nam hoan toan trong khung xanh la.
- Keo tu phai sang trai: Crossing, chon doi tuong nam trong hoac cat qua khung xanh duong.
- Giu Ctrl/Shift de cong them vao tap dang chon.

2. TIM CHU
- Bam Tim chu hoac dung lenh F/FIND.
- Ho tro Chua, Bang dung, Bat dau bang.
- Co the bo qua hoa/thuong va dau tieng Viet.
- Bam Truoc/Tiep hoac lenh FN de chuyen ket qua; ban ve tu zoom va chon doi tuong.

3. DOI XUNG
- Chon mot hoac nhieu doi tuong.
- Bam Doi xung hoac go MI/MIRROR.
- Keo hai diem tao truc doi xung.
- Undo/Redo hoat dong binh thuong.
