DWG SKETCH PWA EDITOR V0.14.0

1. MO TRUC TIEP DXF
- Bam Mo JSON/DXF.
- Chon file .dxf ASCII.
- Ho tro LINE, POLYLINE, LWPOLYLINE, CIRCLE, ARC, ELLIPSE, TEXT, MTEXT.
- Ky tu \U+XXXX trong DXF duoc giai ma thanh tieng Viet.
- Doi tuong chua ho tro duoc thong ke tai muc Doc DXF truc tiep.

2. DIEN THOAI MAN HINH NHO
- Thanh tren chi giu Moi, Mo, Luu.
- Thanh duoi: Mo, Chon, Keo, Tim, Layer, Them.
- Nut Them mo bang cong cu lon de thao tac cam ung.
- Layer va Thuoc tinh la ngan keo, co nut Dong.
- Hai ngon tay zoom/pan; mot ngon tay chon/ve.

3. PWA
- Chay serve_local.cmd tren Windows hoac dua thu muc len HTTPS.
- Mo index.html qua localhost/HTTPS de cai PWA va cap nhat.
